Rod's Sausage Rolls

Ingredients (tap to expand)

  • 500 g beef mince
  • ~300 g sausage meat
  • 1 medium carrot (grated)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)

Method (tap to expand)

  1. Mix both meats with carrot, onion, and seasoning.
  2. Lay out pastry sheets and cut each in half.
  3. Brush edges with egg, roll into logs, cut, and bake.
