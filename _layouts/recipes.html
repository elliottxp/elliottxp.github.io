Rod's Sausage Rolls

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef mince
  • ~300 g sausage meat
  • 1 medium carrot (coarsely grated)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 6 sheets puff pastry
  • Milk or egg wash (for brushing)

Method

Mix both meats in a bowl by hand then add carrot, onion, and seasoning. Mix well. Lay out the pastry sheets and cut each in half. Brush the bottom edge with milk/egg wash. Divide the filling into small orange-sized balls, squeeze each into an elongated shape, and place along the top edge of pastry. Roll carefully, ensuring pastry overlaps. Put each long roll aside and complete all rolls. Cut each roll into short or long sections. Preheat oven, fan-forced 190°C. Lay all rolls on large tray close together. Brush all with egg and milk mixture. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until brown. Serve hot with sauce.

Beef Rump Cap Roast

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg rump cap roast
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
  • Herbs (rosemary, thyme optional)

Method

Preparation (choose one): Marinated Method Place the beef in a heavy-duty plastic bag or container. Add half of 750 ml bottle of stout beer. Seal and refrigerate overnight. Then drink the remaining stout. Dry Rub Method Rub the beef all over with your preferred beef rub or seasoning mix. Cooking Instructions With a sharp knife, make 4–5 deep cuts evenly spaced around the beef. Insert peeled garlic cloves deep into the cuts. Drizzle olive oil over the beef and rub in well with your hands. Place the beef on a roasting rack inside a ceramic or heavy baking dish. Pour water into the dish. Cover the beef tightly with a double layer of foil. Place low in the oven. Cooking Time & Temperature The cooking process can take a minimum of 4 hours (shorter cook): 180°C (no fan) 6–10 hours (slow cook): 160°C (no fan) Finishing About 1 hour before the end of cooking, remove the foil to allow the beef to brown. Remove from the oven and rest for 15–20 minutes before slicing. Use the remaining pan juices to make a rich gravy. Add a little cornflour to thicken. (Optional) I sometime return the sliced beef to the baking dish, cover with gravy, and serve.

Rice Pudding

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Nutmeg to sprinkle

Method

Place rice, milk, sugar, vanilla in greased oven dish. Bake at 160°C, stir occasionally, until rice soft and pudding creamy. Sprinkle nutmeg before serving.

Cheese & Onion Tart

Ingredients

  • 20 cm pie dish (greased)
  • 130 g plain flour
  • Butter/margarine
  • Cold water
  • 2–3 onions
  • 100 g cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Milk/cream
  • Salt, pepper

Method

Make pastry with flour, butter, water. Roll out, line dish. Fry onions until soft, place in pastry shell. Beat eggs with milk/cream, season. Pour over onions. Top with cheese. Bake at 180°C until set and golden.

Blueberry Dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 cups blueberries
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Method

Mix berries, sugar, lemon juice. Chill. Whip cream, fold in gently. Serve layered in glasses.

Wine Trifle

Ingredients

  • 1 sponge cake
  • ½ cup sherry/wine
  • Custard
  • Whipped cream
  • Fruit & jelly

Method

Layer sponge in bowl, pour over wine. Add fruit, jelly, custard. Top with cream. Chill before serving.

Crème Caramel

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Method

Caramelize sugar, pour into dish. Mix eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla. Pour over caramel. Bake in water bath at 160°C 40–45 min. Cool, chill, invert to serve.

Wild Blackberry Conserve

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sugar
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Method

Place berries, sugar, lemon juice in saucepan. Simmer until thickened. Pour into sterilized jars, seal.

Southern Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt, pepper

Method

Mix spices, rub over chicken. Roast 180°C for 1.5h until juices run clear.

Special Baked Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper

Method

Place rice, stock, onion, butter in casserole dish. Bake covered 180°C for 40–45 min until rice tender. Season and serve.

Beef Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 onion
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 2 cups roasted tomatoes
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt, pepper
  • Pasta

Method

Heat oil, sauté onions and celery. Add beef, cook until browned. Stir in tomatoes, simmer 45 min–1h. Add parsley, salt, pepper. Serve with pasta.

Pea & Ham Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup split peas
  • 1 smoked ham hock
  • 1 onion
  • 1–2 carrots
  • Water
  • Salt, pepper

Method

Rinse peas. Place with ham hock, onion, carrots, water. Simmer 1–1.5h. Remove ham, dice, return meat. Season and serve.

Rod's Sticky Pork Ribs

Ingredients

  • 1 rack pork ribs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar

Method

Mix honey, soy, garlic, ginger, sugar. Brush over ribs. Wrap in foil, bake 1.5–2h at 180°C. Unwrap, brush more sauce, broil 5–10 min. Serve hot.

Prune Tart

Ingredients

  • Pastry sheet
  • 250 g pitted prunes
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Butter

Method

Preheat oven 180°C. Line tart pan with pastry. Mix prunes, sugar, cinnamon, eggs, cream. Pour into pastry. Bake 35–40 min until golden. Cool before serving.
